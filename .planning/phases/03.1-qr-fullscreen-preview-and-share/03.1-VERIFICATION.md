## Human Verification Required

### 1. Grow-from-thumbnail Animation Origin

**Test:** Open dashboard, scroll down so a QR row is near the bottom of the viewport. Click its thumbnail.
**Expected:** The QR image appears to scale out from the thumbnail's position (not from screen center), growing to 280px centered. The animation origin should visually match where the thumbnail was.
**Why human:** `transformOrigin` computation is mathematically correct in code, but the perceptual effect of the grow-from-position animation requires visual confirmation, especially when the thumbnail is at a non-centered viewport position.

### 2. Close Animation (Reverse Shrink)

**Test:** Open the preview dialog. Close it three ways: (a) X button, (b) click the dark backdrop, (c) press Escape.
**Expected:** Each dismissal method triggers the `qr-pop-close 200ms ease-in` animation — the QR visibly shrinks back toward the thumbnail position before disappearing.
**Why human:** The close animation depends on `@base-ui/react/dialog` keeping the popup mounted during `data-closed` state for the full 200ms. This lifecycle behavior cannot be verified by static analysis.

### 3. Web Share Button on Mobile

**Test:** Open dashboard on a mobile device or in Chrome with Web Share enabled. Click a QR thumbnail.
**Expected:** A "Share" button appears below the QR image. Tapping it opens the native OS share sheet with the QR URL and label pre-filled.
**Why human:** `navigator.share` availability check cannot be exercised in a non-mobile environment. The guard is correct in code, but the actual share sheet behavior requires a device.

## Gaps Summary

**One lint error introduced by this phase:**

`qr-preview-dialog.tsx:46` — `setTransformOrigin()` is called inside a `useEffect` body, which violates `react-hooks/set-state-in-effect`. ESLint reports this as an error, causing `pnpm lint` to fail with exit code 1.

The code is functionally correct at runtime (the guard `if (open && thumbnailRect)` prevents cascading renders in practice), but the lint rule is treated as an error in this project. The fix is straightforward: derive `transformOrigin` using `useMemo` instead of `useEffect + useState`.

**REQUIREMENTS.md tracking gap:**

PREVIEW-01 through PREVIEW-06 are not registered in REQUIREMENTS.md. They exist in ROADMAP.md and plan frontmatter only. This does not affect functionality but breaks the traceability from requirements to implementation.
