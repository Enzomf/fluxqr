## Decisions Made
- Used `@base-ui/react/dialog` primitives directly (`DialogPrimitive.Root/Portal/Backdrop/Popup`) instead of the shadcn `DialogContent` wrapper — the wrapper bundles its own Portal+Overlay internally, which makes it impossible to provide a custom dark-blur backdrop without fighting the component's structure
- Captured `getBoundingClientRect()` in the `onClick` handler (not in a `useEffect`) — this guarantees the rect reflects the thumbnail's current viewport position at the exact moment of interaction, before any layout shifts from the dialog opening
- Rendered `QrPreviewDialog` per-row inside `QrPulseWrapper` — each row owns its dialog instance; dialogs portal to `<body>` regardless, so no benefit to hoisting state to QrList
